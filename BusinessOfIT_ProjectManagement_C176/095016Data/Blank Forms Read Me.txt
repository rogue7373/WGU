Course title: CompTIA Project+ (Exam PK0-004)
Part Number: 095016

The Blank Forms and Templates folder contains six files for post-class use. These Word and Excel documents are provided to assist you in creating customized project management documents. These files are intended as a helpful tool for your own use. Since project management documents are tailored to fit each organization, these documents are simply provided as a potential starting point, compliments of Logical Operations.

The files included are: 
* Change Control Form Template.docx
* Project Charter Template.docx.
* Project Schedule Template.xlsx
* Project Scope Statement Template.docx
* SOW Template.docx
* Stakeholder Register Template.docx
